## What This Is

QuantumSavory is a GitHub organization for open-source Julia software in quantum information science. The packages cover quantum networks, stabilizer methods, symbolic expressions, optimization, visualization, and backend interfaces.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="QuantumSavory.jl">
    [GitHub](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/QuantumSavory.jl) · [Docs](https://qs.quantumsavory.org/stable/)

    Multi-formalism simulation for quantum hardware, network protocols, noise, timing, and backend selection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="QuantumClifford.jl">
    [GitHub](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/QuantumClifford.jl) · [Docs](https://qc.quantumsavory.org/stable/)

    Stabilizer tableaux, Clifford circuits, graph states, and quantum error-correction workflows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="QuantumSymbolics.jl">
    [GitHub](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/QuantumSymbolics.jl) · [Docs](https://doc.quantumsavory.org/QuantumSymbolics.jl/stable/)

    Symbolic quantum algebra with translation into numerical representations.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Community

QuantumSavory maintains issue bounties, holds weekly office hours, and helps organize the QNumerics summer school.

- [Bounties](/community/bounties/) and [current bounty search](https://github.com/search?q=org%3AQuantumSavory%20label%3A%22bug%20bounty%22%20is%3Aopen&type=issues)
- [Office hours](/community/office-hours/) and the [Julia community events calendar](https://julialang.org/community/#events)
- [QNumerics summer school](https://qnumerics.org/) and the [2026 edition](https://qnumerics.org/2026/)
- [Interactive demos](/start/demos/), including [State Explorer](https://areweentangledyet.com/state_explorer/) and [Repeater Chain Congestion](https://areweentangledyet.com/congestionchain/)

## Host And Funding

The organization is hosted in [Prof. Stefan Krastanov's lab](https://lab.krastanov.org/) at [UMass Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/quantum/).

Development is supported by the [NSF Engineering Research Center for Quantum Networks](https://cqn-erc.arizona.edu/), NSF Cooperative Agreement [1941583](https://api.nsf.gov/services/v1/awards/1941583.json), and NSF Grant [2346089](https://api.nsf.gov/services/v1/awards/2346089.json), Research Infrastructure: CIRC: New: Full-stack Codesign Tools for Quantum Hardware. Bounty payouts are handled through [NumFOCUS](https://numfocus.org/) and the [Julia Krastanov CQN OpenCollective project](https://opencollective.com/julialang/projects/julia-krastanov-cqn).
