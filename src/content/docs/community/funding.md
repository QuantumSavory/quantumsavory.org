# Funding and Host Lab

QuantumSavory is managed at [Prof. Stefan Krastanov's Quantum Information Lab](https://lab.krastanov.org/) at [UMass Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/quantum/). The lab works on modeling, design, control, and optimization of quantum hardware for computation and networking, connecting physical hardware descriptions to error-corrected logical circuits.

Development is supported in part by:

- the [NSF Engineering Research Center for Quantum Networks](https://cqn-erc.arizona.edu/), NSF Cooperative Agreement 1941583,
- NSF Grant 2346089, Research Infrastructure: CIRC: New: Full-stack Codesign Tools for Quantum Hardware.

CQN is a National Science Foundation Engineering Research Center led by the University of Arizona. UMass Amherst researchers contribute to CQN's quantum-network architecture and protocol work.
