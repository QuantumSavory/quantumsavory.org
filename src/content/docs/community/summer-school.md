# Summer School

QuantumSavory helps organize the [QNumerics Summer School on Numerical Methods in Quantum Information Science](https://qnumerics.org/), a hands-on program for QIS researchers who want stronger software and numerical-computing skills.

The current QNumerics edition is planned for June 21-27, 2026 at UMass Amherst. The school combines lectures with hackathons. Topics include:

- general software engineering and cluster tools,
- scientific programming, ODEs, optimization, and autodifferentiation,
- GPU programming,
- wavefunction simulation,
- tensor networks,
- stabilizer formalism and quantum error correction,
- optimal control,
- discrete-event modeling for quantum networks,
- symbolic computer algebra basics.

QuantumSavory.jl appears in the quantum-network simulation sessions. See [qnumerics.org](https://qnumerics.org/2026/) for current dates, registration, logistics, and schedule details.
