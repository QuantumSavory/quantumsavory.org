# Office Hours

Quantum Information Science Office Hours are weekly online sessions for questions about QuantumSavory and related Julia quantum packages, including QuantumClifford.jl, QuantumSymbolics.jl, Gabs.jl, QuantumOptics.jl, and adjacent tools.

Recurring public schedule:

- Fridays, 12:30-1:30 PM Eastern Time
- Online via Zoom
- Public calendar title: Quantum Information Science Office Hours
- Hosted by Prof. Stefan Krastanov and Hana KimLee

Before joining, check the [Julia community events calendar](https://julialang.org/community/#events). That calendar is the public place to confirm whether office hours are happening, rescheduled, or canceled for the week, and it lists the current joining link.

Good topics for office hours include:

- choosing a backend for a simulation,
- getting started with a package,
- discussing a bounty issue,
- debugging a small example,
- checking whether a proposed contribution fits a package.
