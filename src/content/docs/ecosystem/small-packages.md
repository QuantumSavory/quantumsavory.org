# Smaller Packages

These packages fill specific needs around the flagship projects. Some are mature tools. Some are research prototypes. The short descriptions below should be treated as entry points.

## Entanglement Purification

- [BPGates.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/BPGates.jl) ([docs](https://doc.quantumsavory.org/BPGates.jl/)): faster Bell-preserving gate simulation for entanglement-purification circuits.
- [QEPOptimize.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/QEPOptimize.jl) ([demo](https://doc.quantumsavory.org/QEPOptimize.jl/example/pluto.html)): circuit generation and optimization for entanglement purification, built on BPGates.jl.

## Drawing And Interfaces

- [Quantikz.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/Quantikz.jl) ([docs](https://doc.quantumsavory.org/Quantikz.jl/)): draw quantum circuits from Julia with quantikz and TikZ, producing TeX, PDF, PNG, or live previews.
- [PBCCompiler.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/PBCCompiler.jl) ([docs](https://doc.quantumsavory.org/PBCCompiler.jl/)): compiler infrastructure for Pauli-based computation.
- [pyquantumclifford](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/pyquantumclifford): a Python wrapper around QuantumClifford.jl.

## Error Correction

- [QuantumExpanders.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/QuantumExpanders.jl): construction tools for quantum Tanner codes, Ramanujan-graph-based codes, and quantum expander examples.
- [LDPCDecoders.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/LDPCDecoders.jl): LDPC decoding tools, including belief-propagation workflows.
- [PyQDecoders.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/PyQDecoders.jl): Julia wrappers for Python quantum decoders such as PyMatching and related tools.
- [PyTesseractDecoder.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/PyTesseractDecoder.jl): focused wrapper for tesseract-decoder.
- [qECCBenchWiki](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/qECCBenchWiki) ([site](https://eccbench.areweentangledyet.com/)): benchmark wiki for quantum error-correcting codes and decoders.

## Continuous Variables And Graph States

- [Gabs.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/Gabs.jl) ([docs](https://QuantumSavory.github.io/Gabs.jl/)): Gaussian quantum information in phase-space form.
- [Genqo.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/Genqo.jl): Gaussian and non-Gaussian modeling for entangled photon sources.
- [WaveguideQED.jl](https://github.com/qojulia/WaveguideQED.jl) ([docs](https://qojulia.github.io/WaveguideQED.jl/dev/)): time-binned photon wavepacket simulation in waveguide QED.
- [LCOrbits.jl](https://github.com/QuantumSavory/LCOrbits.jl): local-complementation orbit databases for graph states.
